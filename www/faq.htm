FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is BitcoinAverage?

BitcoinAverage.com is the first aggregated bitcon price index being launched in August 2013 with a goal to aggregate rates from all available Bitcoin exchanges around the world and provide a weighted average bitcoin price.
As of March 2014 we have over 30 exchanges integrated and are directly serving over 50000 users monthly plus providing data to numerous other websites and resources.

What is BitcoinAverage Price Index?

BitcoinAverage Price Index is an average rate of all integrated exchanges, weighted by trading volume. Meaning that for every exchange the last trade price and 24 hour sliding window trading volume is considered, and each exchange contributes to the final price only to the extent of it's current trading volume. More details on our infographics explanation page.

Who owns and controls this service?

BitcoinAverage.com is an opensource project, code released under CC-BY-NC-SA 4.0 license. You can view list of contributors and participate yourself at github.
Right now it's managed and developed by a team of two, the lead developer of the project is alexykot.

Who uses BitcoinAverage.com?

  • Android Bitcoin Wallet by Andreas Schildbach - exchange rates
  • Android Bitcoinium price ticker - one of the data sources
  • coingig.com - default bitcoin price
  • kitco.com - bitcoin price and page interface
  • KryptoKit Chrome extension - bitcoin price and charts
  • Price ticker Chrome extension - bitcoin price
  • bitcoinprice.com - prices and website sources
  • and many many more...
